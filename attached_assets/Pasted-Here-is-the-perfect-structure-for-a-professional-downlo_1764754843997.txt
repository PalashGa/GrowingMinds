Here is the perfect structure for a professional downloadable child assessment report for your business.
This covers behavior, IQ, personality, attention, strengths, weaknesses, diet, yoga, improvement plan—everything parents expect.

Use this as your final PDF structure.


---

✅ 1. Cover Page

Child Name

Age

Test Date

Assessment Type

Your Business Logo

Short tagline: “Personalized Child Development Assessment”



---

✅ 2. Executive Summary (1 Page)

A simple explanation of the child’s:

Overall behavior score

Strength areas

Growth areas

3 major recommendations

A quick “Parent Action Checklist”


(Parents love simple summary.)


---

✅ 3. Section: Behavioral Insights (Core)

This is the MOST important for your business.

Include:

✔️ Per-Question Prediction (your 50 predictions)

Q1 → Behavior meaning

Q2 → Behavior meaning

… up to 50


✔️ Final Behavior Domains

Group the 50 questions into 5–6 major behavioral domains:

1. Emotional Regulation


2. Attention & Focus


3. Self-Control / Impulse


4. Social Behavior


5. Confidence & Motivation


6. Stress & Mood Balance



Each domain gets:

Score

Category (Low/Moderate/High)

Graph/Bar chart

Interpretation



---

✅ 4. Section: Strengths Analysis

Very important for parents.
Include:

Academic strengths

Behavioral strengths

Social strengths

Emotional strengths

Cognitive strengths (based on answers, not IQ)


Make it positive and motivating.


---

✅ 5. Section: Areas of Improvement

Gentle, positive tone.

Include:

5–7 focused improvement areas

Why this matters

What patterns indicate these concerns



---

✅ 6. Section: Parent Guidance (Actionable)

This must be simple and practical.

For each key area:

What parents should DO

What parents should AVOID

Suggested communication strategies

Home routines & habits


Example:
“Improve focus: Create a daily 20-min distraction-free study block.”


---

✅ 7. Section: Personalized 7-Day Improvement Plan

Parents value ready-made plans.

Example:

Day 1 — Emotional Control

Breathing exercise + reflecting on feelings.

Day 2 — Focus

Memory game + reduced screen time.

…and so on.


---

✅ 8. Section: 30-Day Growth Plan (Monthly Plan)

Weekly targets:

Week 1: emotions

Week 2: attention

Week 3: habits

Week 4: social and family activities



---

✅ 9. Section: Activity Recommendations

Based on behavioral profile:

Include:

Yoga poses (child friendly)

Brain games

Outdoor activities

Art activities for calmness

Focus exercises

Confidence-building tasks



---

✅ 10. Section: Nutrition Guidance

Basic, non-medical suggestions:

Brain-boosting foods

Concentration foods

Mood-stabilizing foods

Weekly diet tips

What to reduce (sugar, packaged foods, screen-snacks)


(You already have a Nutrition Module — integrate 1 page here.)


---

✅ 11. Section: School Suggestions (Important)

Teachers also use reports.

Add:

Classroom seating suggestions

Homework routine tips

Teacher communication tips

Emotional support needed

Focus strategies for school



---

✅ 12. Progress Tracking Pages

Parents love tracking improvement!

Include tables for:

Week 1–4

Behavior change

Mood

Study habits

Sleep

Focus sessions



---

✅ 13. Final Conclusion Page

Overall positive note

“Your child is unique and capable” message

QR code link to next steps / booking next assessment



---

🟩 BONUS Add-Ons (Optional but Powerful)

⭐ Personality Snapshot

Simple personality traits based on answers.

⭐ Confidence Meter

Graph comparing confidence-related answers.

⭐ Attention Index

Small radar chart.

⭐ Emotional Stability Score

Color-coded graph.

⭐ Parenting Style Recommendation

Based on child behavior (gentle, supportive, structured).


---

🎯 TOTAL REPORT PAGES: 12–25 pages
